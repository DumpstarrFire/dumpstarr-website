Getting Started

Up and running in four steps

Getting Dumpstarr formats into your media stack is straightforward with Profilarr handling the heavy lifting.

01

Install Profilarr

Profilarr is the bridge between Dumpstarr and your *arr apps. Set it up first - it handles syncing formats and profiles automatically.


Dictionarry Website →
02

Link the Database

In Profilarr, point your database source to the Dumpstarr GitHub repository.


03

Pick Your Profiles

Browse the available custom formats and profiles. Select what fits your quality preferences - 4K, 1080p, HDR, audio formats, and more.

04

Sync

Hit sync in Profilarr and your formats are pushed directly into Radarr and Sonarr. That's it - no manual JSON editing required.

Custom formats are scoring rules that Radarr and Sonarr use to rank releases. They match against release names using regex patterns and assign positive or negative scores - letting you automatically prefer or avoid specific codecs, sources, HDR types, groups, and more.
